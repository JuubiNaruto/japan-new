Japan | TownyHavens

Japan is a country on the TownyHavens Minecraft 1:500 scale Earth server. On the server Japan controls most if not all of central japan and our towns can be found up top. All of our towns are unique with our own population and our own stuff. The nation of Japan usually is peaceful unless you disturb us in some way. Diplomatic meetings can be held with the owner to esablish alliances and talk about other threats such as other nations or towns.
